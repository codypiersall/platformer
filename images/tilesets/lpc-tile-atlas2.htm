These assets are not mine,credit goes to all the assets from the LPC contest.

The continuation from the previous atlas the LPC tileset atlas is a 1024x 1024 tileset so you can use it as a a hardware accelerated  texture on even older hardware.

The build_atlas is used for buildings and architecture + some extras

The obj_misk_atlas is the rest of objects and stuff that didn't get into the other atlas.

It is supposed to work with the terrain atlas from previous pack.

Also if by some chance I miss some authors in the license please tell me.
